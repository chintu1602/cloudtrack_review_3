Frequently Asked Questions

You can upload PDF, PNG, JPG, and JPEG files up to 10MB. We support lab reports, prescriptions, and other medical documents. Our Azure AI Document Intelligence service will automatically extract the text content from your documents.

When you generate a diet plan, our system combines the extracted text from your selected documents with your food allergies and medical profile. This information is sent to GPT-4, which analyzes the data and creates a personalized weekly meal plan with foods to eat, foods to avoid, nutritional guidelines, and allergy warnings.

Your food allergies are stored in your profile and are always included in the AI prompt when generating diet plans. The AI is explicitly instructed to never recommend any food that contains your allergens. Allergies are displayed with severity levels (mild, moderate, severe) and specific warnings are included in every diet plan.
